For Donors

Panel

Age Limit

18 -60 years age

Weight

50Kg

Hemoglobin Level

12.5%

Time Interval between each donations

Can donate 3 months once

Body Temperature

37.5 degree Celsius

Affected by Viral Fever

If you are affected by malaria,typhoid before 3 months you are not eligible

Alcohol Consumed

If donor consumed alcohol within the last 24 hours,not eligible

Immunized

If donor undergone any immunization within the past one month,not eligible

Fever/Cold

If you are sick with cold/fever in past 1 week,not eligible

Intaking Medicine

Under treatment with antibiotics or any other medicine, not eligible

Keep it in mind

  • Avoid donating blood in empty stomach
  • Eat three hours before you donate blood
  • Avoid donating blood for 6 months if you had any major surgery

After Donation

  • Have some rest 5 to 20 minutes after donation
  • Do not drive after donation of blood
  • Have some snacks and juice with high sugar content which will help to rejuvenate the blood sugar back Â up
  • Have a good meal with high protein content such as Chicken, Fish etc
  • Do not consume Alcohol for 8 hours after donation of blood
  • Avoid going for a heavy body works such as gym, dancing, running etc at least for next one day
